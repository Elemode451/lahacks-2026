## Design Context

### Users
Music enthusiasts and neuroscience-curious listeners who want to understand the brain science behind their listening habits. Used in a focused desktop session — not mobile-first. The emotional context is contemplative, curious, and slightly clinical.

### Brand Personality
**Precise · Introspective · Alive**
Scientific precision without coldness. The interface should feel like a high-end neuroscience tool that also feels poetic. Think: MRI scan meets audiophile listening journal.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light — cream (#fffdf5) background, not pure white. Dark navy (#0d3b66) for all primary type. Salmon/orange (#f95738) as the sole accent.
- **Visual tone**: Apple-inspired restraint with scientific detail. Lots of breathing room, subtle micro-treatments. No flashy gradients, no glassmorphism everywhere.
- **References**: Apple Health app meets linear.app meets a beautifully typeset science paper.
- **Anti-references**: No dark mode glowing accents, no purple-to-blue gradient "AI aesthetics", no blocky card grids.
- **Motion**: Framer Motion with spring physics for panels, linear opacity fades for content reveals.

### Design Principles
1. **Space is the primary design tool** — generous whitespace signals quality; crowding signals panic.
2. **One accent, used sparingly** — #f95738 appears only where it matters; navy carries everything else.
3. **Hierarchy through weight and space, not color** — only escalate to color when weight + space aren't enough.
4. **Scientific restraint** — data is presented clearly without decorative flourish; the brain visualization is the spectacle.
5. **Cream is not white** — #fffdf5 is warm and intentional; preserve it everywhere, never substitute a neutral gray.
